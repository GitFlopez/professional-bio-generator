## Target Buyer Personas

### Persona 1: Career Changer
IT director pivoting to consulting. Needs a bio that positions for the next chapter, not the last one. Searches: "professional bio writer," "LinkedIn bio consultant," "consultant bio template."

### Persona 2: New Speaker
Coach or consultant applying to conferences for the first time. Has no one-sheet. Doesn't know what goes on it. Searches: "speaker one-sheet template," "how to apply to speak at conference," "speaker bio example."

### Persona 3: Freelancer Launching
New to Upwork or Fiverr. Profile bio is generic. Needs to look established before they are. Searches: "freelance profile bio," "Upwork bio examples," "how to write a consultant bio."

### Persona 4: Executive in Transition
VP-level executive building advisory board positions or board seat applications. Needs a polished 200-word media bio that doesn't sound self-promotional. High willingness to pay, low price sensitivity at $29.
